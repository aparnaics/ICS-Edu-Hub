SPOKEN ENGLISH

Spoken English refers to the ability to communicate fluently and effectively in English through speaking. It involves various aspects such as pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, sentence structure, and conversational skills. Spoken English is an essential skill for personal, professional, and academic success in English-speaking environments.
