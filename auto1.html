AUTOCAD

AutoCAD is one of the most widely used software tools for computer-aided design (CAD), primarily for drafting and designing in various industries such as architecture, engineering, construction, and interior design. It is developed by Autodesk and allows professionals to create precise 2D and 3D drawings, models, and blueprints for a variety of projects.
