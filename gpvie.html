GLASS PAINTING

    Glass painting is an artistic technique where paint is applied to a glass surface to create a design or decoration. It is a popular form of decorative art used for items like windows, panels, glassware, and even framed artworks. The translucency of glass adds a unique dimension to the artwork, creating effects that differ from traditional painting surfaces.
