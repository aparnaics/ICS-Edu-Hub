CORELDRAW

CorelDRAW is a powerful vector graphics editor developed by Corel Corporation. It is widely used by graphic designers, illustrators, and artists to create a variety of digital artwork, including logos, brochures, illustrations, and other visual elements. CorelDRAW is known for its intuitive interface, comprehensive toolset, and flexibility, making it a popular alternative to Adobe Illustrator.
