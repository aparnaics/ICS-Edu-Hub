TALLY

Tally is a popular accounting software used by businesses for managing finances, accounting, inventory, payroll, and other business operations. It is widely used for small and medium-sized businesses to keep track of financial transactions and generate reports like balance sheets, profit & loss statements, and VAT returns. The software is known for its ease of use and its ability to handle complex accounting tasks.
