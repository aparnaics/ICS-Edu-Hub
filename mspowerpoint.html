MS POWERPOINT


    Microsoft PowerPoint is a powerful presentation software developed by Microsoft, primarily used to create slideshows composed of text, images, charts, multimedia, and animations. It is part of the Microsoft Office suite and is available for both Windows and MacOS. PowerPoint is widely used for creating presentations in business, education, and various other fields.
