SAP

SAP stands for Systems, Applications, and Products in Data Processing. It is a leading enterprise resource planning (ERP) software platform that helps businesses manage their operations, finances, human resources, supply chains, customer relations, and more in an integrated way. SAP provides various software applications that automate and streamline business processes, making it easier for companies to gather data, analyze it, and make informed decisions.
