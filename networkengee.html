NETWORK ENGINEERING

Network Engineering is a field of engineering that focuses on the design, implementation, management, and maintenance of computer networks. These networks allow devices to communicate and share data, such as local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), and even large-scale systems like the internet.
