SAGE 50


    Sage 50 is a comprehensive accounting and business management software solution designed for small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs). It is part of the Sage Group, a leading global provider of enterprise resource planning (ERP) and financial software solutions. Sage 50 is often used to manage financial tasks like accounting, invoicing, inventory, payroll, and reporting. It offers a robust set of tools tailored to the needs of SMBs in various industries.

    • Pricing and Plans
