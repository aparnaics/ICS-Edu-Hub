FABRIC PAINTING

Fabric painting is the art of applying paint to fabric in a way that creates a permanent design. It is widely used in fashion, home décor, and craft projects, allowing for the transformation of plain textiles into artistic expressions. The process involves using specialized paints, techniques, and tools to create designs on fabric.
