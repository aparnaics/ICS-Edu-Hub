MS WORD


    Microsoft Word (MS Word) is a powerful word processing software developed by Microsoft. It is part of the Microsoft Office suite of productivity tools, which also includes Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and others. MS Word is widely used for creating, editing, formatting, and printing text-based documents.
